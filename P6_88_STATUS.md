# P6.88 — As-Filed Fundamental Components

Registered v1 components:

- revenue growth YoY;
- operating margin and YoY margin change;
- net margin;
- cash conversion;
- accrual quality;
- leverage;
- share-count growth/dilution;
- capex intensity and asset growth as diagnostics.

Primary values come from SEC face financials. Only comparable 10-Q single-quarter and 10-K annual
periods are admitted in v1; cumulative Q2/Q3 cash-flow values are not silently treated as discrete quarters.
