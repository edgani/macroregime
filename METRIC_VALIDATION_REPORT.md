# Metric validation summary — v4.2

Current metric grades are stored in `metric_grades.json`. None of those grades grants autonomous direction or capital permission.

- `PARTIAL` metrics may appear only as descriptive or banded research context.
- `REJECTED` metrics may not emit a directional signal.
- `FEED_GATED` and `RESEARCH_PROXY` metrics remain blocked until their exact point-in-time data contracts and proof gates pass.
- Only exact `PRODUCTION` or `LIMITED_PRODUCTION` grades could become eligible, and the shipped proof registry contains zero such promoted predictive components.
