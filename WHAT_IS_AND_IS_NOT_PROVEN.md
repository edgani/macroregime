# What War Room OS v4.2 proves—and does not prove

## Proven by the packaged software tests

- The application compiles, renders, starts, and remains fail-closed.
- All eight workspaces and five market adapters return reviewable content or an explicit missing-data state.
- Generic OHLCV rows are descriptive price context, not directional selectors.
- IHSG is long-only.
- Options analytics are gated by actual product/instrument/feed capability.
- Public option-chain OI does not create a signed dealer regime without explicit dealer inventory sign.
- Technical reference targets are separated from fair value and alpha potential.
- Alpha scenario values and expected value are withheld when economic inputs or calibrated probabilities are missing.
- The HMM/Wasserstein model is a blocked challenger in a baseline tournament, not a production winner.
- Current-development date freshness is separated from human source verification.
- No component can obtain capital permission from compile, UI, parser, synthetic, or historical demonstration tests.

## Not proven by this package

- Predictive edge, profitable long/short selection, target accuracy, or future alpha.
- That any formula or weight survives point-in-time repeated walk-forward testing after costs.
- That the Wasserstein HMM paper result replicates across vendors, windows, universes, constraints, or untouched lockbox data.
- Dealer ownership, beneficial-owner intent, or institutional intent from public flow/OI/prints.
- Net profitability, capacity, live execution quality, or prospective stability.
- Production eligibility for any predictive component.
