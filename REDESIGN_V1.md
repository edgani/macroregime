# Capital Intelligence Map — Redesign V1

The redesign turns the previous collection of panels into one zoomable decision path:

`World → Regime → Market → Theme/Bottleneck → Ticker/Token → Institutional Positioning → Execution → Validation`

Every workspace uses the same frame: command bar, navigation, intelligence canvas, Decision Rail, evidence ledger, decision queue and live event tape. The visual type changes by task rather than forcing every page into the same graph.

## Workspaces

1. Mission Control — global state to actionable market path.
2. Macro & Regime — driver-to-regime transmission.
3. Early Warning — risk propagation and confirmation sequence.
4. Alpha Center — structural opportunity constellation.
5. Institutional Positioning — options, TRF, SEC and on-chain evidence.
6. US Stocks — regime, sector, flow and ticker funnel.
7. IHSG — EM/FX, foreign flow, broker/controller and long-only outputs.
8. Crypto — liquidity, beta rotation, Smart Money and labeled transfers.
9. Commodities — physical supply, curve, COT and equity beneficiaries.
10. FX — policy, rate differential, funding and cross-market transmission.
11. Flow & Rotation — front-run/current/late/exhausted routes.
12. Supply Chain — trigger, bottleneck and value-capture chain.
13. Company Intel — thesis, fundamentals, positioning, valuation and kill thesis.
14. Knowledge Graph — typed causal edges and lag.
15. Validation Center — production/research/rejected status with OOS requirements.

## Anti-confusion rules

- Default `OBSERVED` mode can show only tickers that cleared a market/setup path.
- Structural Alpha candidates appear in `ALL LAYERS`, not as live Mission Control actions.
- One ticker object and one evidence registry feed all tabs.
- Missing source means no actionable ticker, not a substituted narrative.
- IHSG remains long-only.
- Colors describe state; they are not direct buy/sell instructions.
