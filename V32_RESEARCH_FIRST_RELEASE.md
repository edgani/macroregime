# v3.2 Research-First Release

## Design decision

The previous 16-item flat navigation made a single decision span too many disconnected
screens. v3.2 uses eight workspaces, a persistent Decision Rail and a six-card research
dock. Detail remains available through 19 subviews without turning every function into
a top-level tab.

## Research kernel in every market

Each market adapter contains:

- important decision problem;
- epistemic state;
- Current Rain and recognition gap;
- structural pressure and trigger;
- Study the Tape winner/loser autopsy;
- condition persistence;
- edge decomposition;
- Counterparty Challenge;
- Cost of Staying and Value of Waiting;
- entry, trade invalidation, thesis invalidation and dynamic exit;
- market-specific strong baseline and next cheapest falsifier.

## Backend semantic corrections

- Broker flow is now route-level `NET_BUY_CONTEXT`, `NET_SELL_CONTEXT` or balanced
  context. Beneficial owner, intent, crossing and facilitation remain unverified.
- Legacy market-cap bull/base/bear priors, EV, alpha tiers and suggested weights are
  withheld. A calibrated company-level model is required.
- Knowledge-graph paths produce a research inventory, not a “best equity.”
- Legacy BUY/SELL recommendations are converted to triggered watches, watch states,
  reduce/avoid or no-trade. IHSG short execution remains prohibited.

## What is actually validated

- Python and JavaScript contracts;
- browser rendering of eight workspaces and 19 subviews;
- zero node overlaps in the deterministic UI fixture;
- staged bootstrap and offline exit from INITIALIZING;
- arrow lineage and no-data blocking;
- provider parsers and live-stack fixture semantics;
- GCFIS regression suite;
- a real Streamlit health endpoint;
- fail-closed semantic controls above.

## What is not claimed

This release does not claim that all components have positive out-of-sample edge, that
full point-in-time WFA has passed, or that mature prospective P&L exists. Those remain
inside the validation and capital-permission ladder rather than being hidden behind the UI.
